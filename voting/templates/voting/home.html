Engineered for absolute trust.

Traditional databases are vulnerable to silent tampering. ChainVote utilizes decentralized cryptography to guarantee mathematical integrity without compromising voter privacy.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Your identity is hashed instantly. We verify that you are eligible to vote without ever storing or knowing who you actually are.

Immutable Ledger

Every vote is mathematically chained to the previous one using SHA-256. If a single vote is altered, the entire chain instantly breaks.

Active Threat SOC

A live Security Operations Center (SOC) dashboard that monitors, logs, and blocks duplicate voting attempts and network anomalies in real-time.

The Protocol.

Three steps. Zero knowledge. Absolute security.

1

Authenticate

Enter your Aadhaar or Roll ID. It is hashed client-side immediately. We never store your raw identity on our servers.

2

Encrypt

Select your candidate. The payload is instantly secured using military-grade AES-256 encryption before it ever leaves your device.

3

Mine & Verify

The vote is committed to the blockchain. You receive a verifiable cryptographic receipt to ensure your vote was counted.
